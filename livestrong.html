LIVESTRONG.org is trying to move past the lost of Lance, and create awareness for what the Foundation currently does via a new product, service, or communication. After talking to the core user group, and doing secondary research on related blogs, we addressed these problems by designing a new application to live on the LIVESTRONG.org site. It would allow cancer patients to enter in their personal preferences, and schedule of treatments. Once inside the application, they could invite others to their preference circles, and ask for help throughout their treatment.

Role: design, user experience, strategy.
