UDR

For a two day design sprint, we worked through the online apartment searching experience for UDR. We did user testing and research to redefine the experience of finding a new home. We focused on giving the user a way to choose their surroundings and neighborhood, since the majority of the people in our focus groups already knew what amenities their future apartment needed to include.

Role: design, strategy, user experience.
