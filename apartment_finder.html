Apartment finder

People in their twenties continuously find themselves looking for a new apartment. After talking to a user group, we found the process of looking for a new home is unintuitive, and at times unpleasant. We created a paper prototype to test out a new user flow for an apartment finder mobile application.

Role: design, user experience, strategy.
