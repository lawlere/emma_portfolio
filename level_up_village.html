Level Up Village

Level Up Village is a non-profit that inspires global citizenship through Science, Technology, Engineering and Math (STEM) based learning opportunities.

Their main problem is that they do not have a high level of awareness outside of their personal communities, mainly through word of mouth. We conducted interviews and surveys for their 4 main user groups: moms, teachers, kids in the program, and the people who run the program.

Throughout the research phase, we consulted with the director of Level Up Village. In the end, we presented this online brand book so they had a consistent branding and messaging across all their content.
