Dollar Tree Site Redesign

During a two day design sprint, we were asked to strategize about the mobile and desktop experience of Dollar Tree. After diving into secondary research on the core user of Dollar Tree, we reorganized their content into six communities, and simplified the access points as much as possible. Instead of focusing simply on the discounted bulk products, we narrowed in on the communities that Dollar Tree already has original content for.

Role: design, strategy.
